Fitness Partner

Conference Pingendo

Give you a better private gym enviroment.

Vision

Nowadays indoor fitness becomes more normally around us. Through the long-term research and analysis. Scientist find that playing music while exercising can improve the result of fitness. It’s not only makes the speed of metabolism, burning of fat faster but also exercise the endurance of the practitioner, improve self-confidence.
By the way, different exercising should match up different music rhythm. Consider to people who have no enough time to go to the Gym after work or study, Fitness Partner (F.P.) creating a good atmosphere while you doing fitness at home. According to the heart beats of user, the system can play the different music with suitable rhythm. All the music are divided to different kinds. 
At the same time, each kind of music provide suitable light around the space in order to make user feel more relaxed and enjoy the fitness. F.P. system provide the distinctive fitness experience different from the Gym in your private space. It is suited for all the people who prefer to do fitness at home.

AMI Steps

Sensing

Detect the daily heartbeat rate of user by smart band. Use heartbeat sensor to measure the real-time heartbeat.

Reasoning

Exercising while listening music improve the result of fitness. According to the heartbeat of user, F.P provide the more suitable music during the exercising, The colors of lights in environment change with heartbeat as user like in order to know the state of exercise clearly. It also create a good atmosphere during the fitness.

Interacting 

Provides the users a mobile application to start the fitness. Customize the total time of fitness, including the warm-up time and the initial light color in different sections. Show the real-time heartbeat rate of user. Auto-play music and change the colors of light in the different sections according to the heartbeat rate of user. Remind user time out or continue the fitness.

Acting

The system is able to control LEDS' color. Change or pause the music as user like in the current section of heartbeat rate.

AMI Features

Sensitive

The system can sense the daily of user before fitness and real-time heartbeat rate during the fitness.

Responsive

The system will change the music and lights' color by detecting users' heartbeat rate in real time.

Transparent

It can an be easily installed in any private gym.

Adaptive

People who prefer to do fitness in a private space and improve the feeling during the fitness. It only works for one user privately at the same time.

System Architecture

Hardware architecture

  • Computational nodes: 
     Computer: To store data required and analyzing data to perform some action.

  • Sensors: 
    Smart bracelet: To detect the heart beat rate of the user.

  • Actuators: 
    Speakers: To play the sound. 
    Smart LEDS: To light the room with different color.

  • Interface: 
    Smart Phones: Using an application which can let users change the music and light color.

Software architecture:

  • Smart Phone Application: 
    Display real-time heartbeat rate. Show the what the music is playing and can be changed by user’s preferences. 

  • Server:
    It stores the music and the user’s heartbeat data by database.
       
  • Computer Programs: 
    Analyzing the data and Controlling the speaker and LEDS. 

Purpose and scope

The Purpose of our system is that improve the result of fitness(speed of metabolism, burning of fat faster ) and create a good atmosphere for the indoor fitness with music and light.
Firstly, the system recognizes the specific user and receive the rest heartbeat rate the last one week from user’s smart band to define the initial data.
User starts to warm-up, the system receives the command to play the random suitable music in warm-up heartbeat rate.At the same time,the system counting down with the value user set before.
It started the official fitness state when the warm-up is time out.According to the heartbeat rate during the fitness,the system compare the data with the initial data to match up the music from users’ data base and light suit for the change of rate.
The colors changing of LED has 3 sections as music playing(anaerobic exercise,aerobic exercise,maximum heartbeat rate state).The colors changing of LED with the purpose of making users know about their sate of exercise directly.It will be a good atmosphere during the fitness. It also can define the colors of light and music as user like any time.
The system can be control to stop or not any time.At the end of fitness,the music stop playing and the light turn off.
